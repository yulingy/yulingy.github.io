Yuling Yan

 

I am a Norbert Wiener Postdoctoral Associate in the Institute for Data, Systems and Society at MIT, mentored by Professor Philippe Rigollet and Professor Martin Wainwright.

Previously, I obtained my Ph.D. degree at Princeton University in 2023, advised by Professor Yuxin Chen and Professor Jianqing Fan. I received my bachelor's degree from Peking University in 2018.

I will join Department of Statistics at University of Wisconsin-Madison as an assistant professor in Fall 2024.

Research interests: high-dimensional statistics, non-convex optimization, reinforcement learning, optimal transport as well as their applications in economics and social sciences.
